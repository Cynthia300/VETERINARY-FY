Frequent questions

These are the most common questions that clients ask us.

What is a veterinary clinic?

The veterinary clinic, unlike the veterinary hospital, is a private entity that focuses mainly on outpatient treatments for animals and private consultations. It is a specialty of veterinary practice that deals with diseases and their prevention, management, behavior, nutrition, genetic selection, preventive and curative medicine, identification and examination of dogs, cats and other companion animals.

What can this veterinary offer me?

We can offer you services such as digital radiology, ultrasound, canine and feline daycare, hairdressing, trichina analysis, preventive medicine, analysis in our own Laboratory, new companion animals, obstetrics and reproduction, dermatology, internal medicine, feline medicine, soft tissue surgery, ophthalmology and laser surgery.

Do you have a veterinary hospital?

Yes, we have a veterinary hospital to which we refer animals or pets that we cannot care for in a basic veterinary clinic.

Are you going to allow us to help your pet?

If you want us to care for or assist your pet with any of the information we offer, do not hesitate to contact us and tell us what type of appointment you require.
